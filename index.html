How To Play Go Fish

Objective:

The objective of Go Fish is to match pairs of fish cards until all 27 different species have been identified.


Gameplay:

  1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game. This will shuffle and deal a set of fish cards onto the game board.
  2. Each fish card contains an image of a fish species along with its name.
  3. To play, click on two cards to flip them over. If the cards match, they will remain face up. If they don't match, they will flip back over.
  4. Continue flipping pairs of cards until all 27 pairs have been matched
  5. As you correctly match pairs, you'll notice the progress bar fill up. The game automatically deals another set of cards each time you match all 9 pairs in a dealt hand.
  6. The game ends when all 27 pairs have been matched.

Tips

  • Pay attention to the names and images of the fish species to improve your memory and speed.
  • The more familiar you become with the fish species, the faster you'll complete the game.
  • Use the "Pause" button to pause the timer if needed, and "Resume" to continue playing.

Enjoy playing Go Fish and have fun learning about Ireland's diverse fish species !
